Nikon FM10 ?

Nikon FM10

Exposure control : Manual [M]
Shutter speed
: Mechanical 1/2,000 to 1 s, plus B (long exposure)
Power source : One 3V CR1/3N lithium battery, or two 1.55V SR44 silver-oxide batteries, or two 1.5V LR44 alkaline batteries
